# Code of Conduct – HumanLink

## Our Commitment

HumanLink is built to connect people who need help with people who can help.  
We are committed to keeping this platform respectful, safe, and positive.

---

## Expected Behavior

All contributors and users must:

- Be respectful to others
- Communicate clearly and politely
- Help others without expecting harm or abuse
- Respect different backgrounds and opinions
- Focus on constructive collaboration

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following is not allowed:

- Harassment, insults, or hate speech
- Fake or spam help requests
- Misuse of karma system
- Trolling or misleading users
- Sharing harmful or illegal content

---

## Karma System Integrity

HumanLink uses karma to build trust.

Do NOT:

- Create fake accounts to boost karma
- Exchange fake reviews
- Abuse reward system

Violations may result in suspension or removal.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you see misuse or abuse:

- Open an issue on GitHub
- Or contact project maintainers

---

## Our Goal

To build a real-world network of trust, help, and human connection.
